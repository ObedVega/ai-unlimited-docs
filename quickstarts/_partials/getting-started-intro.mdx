## Overview
This how-to shows how to gain access to a Teradata database by running it on your local machine. Once you finish the steps you will have a working Teradata Vantage Express database on your computer.

:::note
Starting with version 17.20, Vantage Express includes the following analytics packages: [Vantage Analytics Library](https://docs.teradata.com/r/Enterprise_IntelliFlex_Lake_VMware/Vantage-Analytics-Library-User-Guide), [Bring Your Own Model (BYOM)](https://docs.teradata.com/r/Enterprise_IntelliFlex_Lake_VMware/Teradata-VantageTM-Bring-Your-Own-Model-User-Guide), [API Integration with AWS SageMaker](https://docs.teradata.com/r/Enterprise_IntelliFlex_VMware/Teradata-VantageTM-API-Integration-Guide-for-Cloud-Machine-Learning).
:::
